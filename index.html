ABOUT US
United Comprehensive High School, Wasimi is a secondary school in Ogun State, Nigeria. It is currently located at Wasimi, Ewekoro LGA, Ogun State. The aim of this School is to provide an all round formation of students and to impart sound education by inculcating healthy study habits, discipline, self-reliance and moral values . . .

PRINCIPAL'S DESK
PRINCIPAL'S DESK
Welcome to United Comprehensive High School, Wasimi Ewekoro LGA, Ogun State. I am happy to accompany you in the Educational Journey through the various departments, activities, scholastic and non scholastic life of our children who are entrusted to us by the parents. Our methodology of education is not to produce little academic monsters, dehumanized, nor is it to produce pious faithful, allergic to the world they live in, incapable of responding to it sympathetically. . . .
